Dieter Rams:

10 Principles for
Good Design

1

Good design
is easy to
understand

A clear design that could make
the user experience simple and
easy for anyone.

2

Good design
is inclusive

No one group should be felt like
they've been left out and/or are
able to appreciate design that
was meant for another.

3

Good design
is simplistic

Sometimes less is better.
Too much can be confusing.

4

Good design
is aesthetic

If one was to see a product
daily then it's important
to ensure that the design
doesn't fatigue the viewer.

5

Good design
tells a story

A small sample of the
design/thought process
should be visable
throughout the work itself.

6

Good design
is influential

Other designers may want
to mimic anothers successful
work in their own.

7

Good design
has morals

Design is commonly used to send
a message; what that message is
reflectes a designers own morals.

8

Good design is
environmentally
friendly

Design makes an important
contribution to the preservation of
the environment. It conserves
resources and minimises physical
and visual pollution throughout
the lifecycle of the product.

9

Good design
is stable

It ensures a product has longevity
to it without any compromise to
its stability and avoids any
unnecessary design elemments.

10

Good design
is honest

It does not make a product
more innovative, powerful or
valuable than it really is. It does
not attempt to manipulate
the consumer with promises
that cannot be kept.
